1. LOCAL BHUJUNG HOME
ADDRESS: Bhujung village, Lamjung
INTERVIEWEES: Khem Bahadur Gurung + Madan Singh Gurung

KODHO SEL ROTI (MILLET BREAD): Mix kodho (millet flour), caulo, sugar and water.
Deep-fry for two minutes. 🇳🇵ALOO TARKARI (POTATO CURRY): Cut potatoes, chilis, tomato, and garlic. Add soya bean oil to heat in a pan. Add fenugreek seeds, potatoes, and water.
Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Add more water, turmeric, jira masala, salt, potatoes, and onions. Add water and other vegetables. Cook for another 10-15 minutes.
